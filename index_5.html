Profile

Six years across power generation, pharmaceutical, and chemical operations — including hands-on engineering on Bangladesh's first ultra-supercritical coal power plant.

Shohag is currently Deputy Manager in the Operation & Maintenance department at BIFPCL, promoted from Assistant Manager in July 2026. He directs preventive and predictive maintenance strategy for the coal handling plant of a 1,320 MW ultra-supercritical facility, oversees work-permit and HSE compliance, and leads cross-functional coordination between operations, maintenance, and utilities teams.

Before BIFPCL, he built a foundation across pharmaceutical utilities (Renata Limited) and industrial chemical operations (Samuda Chemical Complex) — commissioning HVAC, chillers, compressors, and fire-protection systems, and running root-cause-driven reliability programs. He's PMP certified, proficient in AutoCAD, SolidWorks, and HAP, and is now working toward the next phase of his career: MEP design and consultancy for buildings.
